Team Process Management

Meetings

As of creation of the charter, most team meetings outside of class will be coordinated via Slack.

Our meetings will be designed around the five for five.

  • The first five minutes recapping where the team is to date.
  • The second five minutes will cover the meeting agenda.
  • The third five minutes will be open to the group incase we are missing something for the meeting.
  • The second to last five minutes will be spent recapping highlights.
  • The last five minutes will map out ongoing work, future milestones, and next team communication time.

Decision Making Procedures

  • We will first seek a general consensus, keeping in mind the needs of the client.
  • Next we will rely on the professional judgement of the role leader. In a situation where there is an impasse the project manager will seek an objective opinion and resolve the issue.

Team Communication

  • Team members will provide basic contact information to other team members. A "contact" channel in DreamTeam206 is provided for that purpose.
  • From time to time, team members will find opportunities to meet, talk, or text in groups of two or three, without all team members present.
  • Team members are expected to notify the full team about any significant decisions or actions resulting from such communications. If for some reason the full team can not be notified, then the Project Manager must be notified.

Expectations

Attendance

  • Attend meetings on time, and communicate immediately via Slack or cell if a team member will be late or cannot attend.

Participation

  • Team members are expected to contribute all thoughts and ideas.

  • Every idea matters and should be discussed.
