Welcome to the Clark County Bicycle Safety Quiz!

This is the official Clark Communities Bicycle and Pedestrian Advisory Committee's Bicycle Safety Quiz.

How is your bicycle safety knowledge? Test it here to find out!

Learn to share the road safely and knowledgeably by advancing through the quiz from Bronze to Silver to Gold. You can finish one level or all three.

At the end, you can print out a completion certificate with your name and the level you completed written on it. Navigate to the quiz by clicking the arrow below.
