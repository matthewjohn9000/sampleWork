Here's How to Start!

There are three quiz levels - Bronze (12 questions), Silver (7 questions), Gold (6 questions). For each question, click on the best choice and then click the Submit button under the answer options.

When the quiz is completed you will see which answers were correct and why, as well as hints for the incorrect answers to improve your score next time.

When you finish at least one level, you can print a Certificate of Completion, if you score above a 70%. You may also return home at any time by clicking the logo on the bottom of the screen.

Good Luck!
